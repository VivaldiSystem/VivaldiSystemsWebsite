How to Pick Up a Ticket

This tutorial will guide you through the steps on how to pick up a ticket after you've cleaned the items and marked the ticket ready.

Follow these steps to continue

Step 1: Tap on the PickUp Ticket icon.

Click or tap on the Pick Up button on the main screen of Follow POS to load up the customer search window.

Step 2: Select Customer

Click on the search text field on the top and enter the customer's name or account number (which usually is the customer's phone number) either entirely or partially. Vivaldi will recognize even just part of your input and it will locate the closest to what you typed in.

You can also type in the ticket number and then click the "pickup by ticket" button on the right. This is convenient if you want to speed up the process or if your customer seems to be in a hurry.

After pressing the "search now" button, highlight the customer from the list and press "pickup ticket" from the left pane.

Step 3: Checkmark Selected Tickets

This is the screen that shows the list of the selected customer's ticket that are either in-process or ready status.

You will also notice that they tickets that are either racked or that have been marked ready have a checkmark applied to the row on the left allowing you for faster pickup. You'll know that if the ticket with items are racked if the status is "R" for ready and that a location has been included.

Any tickets that have a check mark clicked on will be picked up whether their status is marked ready or not.

If you want to know what items are in the ticket, click on the ticket number and Vivaldi will automatically display the contents within the ticket on the left side.

Select the tickets that are ready by placing a checkmark next to the line and then clicking "$ Cashout Selected Tickets." This prompts you to a new window.

--->

Step 4: Select Payment Type

You have the ability to pay in check, cash, credit card, account, or house account. Use the appropriate button.

If the customer pay's with cash, you can use the quick cash buttons on top (like a 10 or 20 dollar bill), click on the right button, press cash, and then you can give out the change.

If you have a cashdrawer, make sure you have the cashdrawer checkbox turned on before pressing the cash button.

Press "OK" and this will lead you back to the main menu where you can do your next order of business.

You’ve Picked Up a Ticket!
