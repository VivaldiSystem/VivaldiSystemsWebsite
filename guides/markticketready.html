Mark Tickets Ready and Assign Location

This tutorial will guide you through the steps on how to mark tickets ready and assign location for racking after you've written the ticket and cleaned the items.

If you assign tickets to the rack by number, then you’re gonna “assign location”. If you mark tickets by alpha or something other than by number, you’re gonna want to “mark tickets ready.”

Mark Tickets Ready

Follow these steps to mark tickets ready. Scroll down to learn how to assign locations

Step 1: Click on the Mark Tickets Ready icon.

Click or tap on the mark tickets ready button from the main screen of Follow POS to load up the module in order to change the status.

Step 2: Input the Ticket Number

Once the screen pops up, this is where you’ll input the ticket number. If you manually are entering the numbers using the keyboard, after you're done typing the number, press enter on your keyboard or click on the "mark tickets ready" button on the window.

If you have a barcode reader, you’ll simply scan the each ticket’s barcode. After you scan, it will automatically press enter.

You’ll notice that it gives a running total of the tickets you’ve marked ready. This screen updates automatically as you continue scanning tickets.

If you enter a ticket that doesn’t exist or you’ve mis-scanned something else, this screen will also warn you that the ticket is not found on the screen below.

If you have email turned on in your system, Follow POS will automatically send an email to the customer stating that their items are cleaned.

Assign Location

Follow these steps to assign locations

Step 1: Click on the Assign Location Icon

You don’t need to use mark tickets ready if you’re doing this option, since assigning a location will mark the tickets ready in the process.

Press the Assign Location button on the main menu, which will lead you to a new window where you can type in the ticket number

Step 2: Input the Ticket Number

Here you can either scan the tickets using a barcode scanner and then scanning the location or you can use the keyboard and type in the ticket number, press enter, then type in the location, then press enter. The system will then assign the ticket number to the location.

If you have email turned on in your store, it will automatically send an email to the customer saying that the tickets ready.

Vivaldi will list ticket by the same customer when you scan one so you can see if the customer has other tickets on the rack and it will show the rack location so you can group them together.

After you're done, press "OK" and this will lead you back to the main menu where you can do your next order of business.

You’ve Now Learned How to Mark Tickets Ready and Assign Location!
